Title: Need For Speed: Interstellar

Elevator Pitch:
The classic game of Need For Speed but in space. Many of the rules will be the same but the cars will
react differently on the roads due to the lack of gravity.  

World Background:
The roads are the rings of the planets. Players race around the planets with astroids and space debris as obstacles for them. 

Pillars:




Gameplay Description:
The cars are lighter and players will have to rely on drifting skills to progress through the race.
